About Our Practice

Dedicated to excellence in dental care for over two decades

Dr. KD Hariparsad is an alumnus of the University of Witwatersrand, having graduated with her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) in 2002. With over 23 years of experience in the dental field, she brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to her practice.

A three-time winner at the prestigious Best of Newcastle Awards, Dr. Hariparsad has been consistently recognised for her exceptional dental care and patient satisfaction. Passionate about her craft, she stays at the forefront of dental advancements through continuous education and training. Her commitment to patient care is evident in her gentle approach and attention to detail, ensuring each patient receives personalised treatment tailored to their needs.

Our practice philosophy centres on providing comprehensive dental care in a comfortable environment, where patient education and preventive care are prioritised alongside exceptional treatment outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the most common questions our patients ask.

Do you accept medical aid?

Yes, Dr. Hariparsad accepts cash and most major South African medical aids including Discovery, Bonitas, Momentum, and others. Please contact us to verify your specific medical aid coverage.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, some patients may need more frequent visits based on their individual oral health needs.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

For dental emergencies (severe pain, knocked-out tooth, swelling, or trauma), please call us immediately at 034 312 1690 or WhatsApp 074 711 3339. We prioritise emergency cases and will do our best to see you as soon as possible.

How can I overcome dental anxiety?

We specialise in gentle dentistry for anxious patients. Options include:

  • Thorough explanation of procedures to reduce fear of the unknown.
  • Taking breaks during treatment when needed.
  • Using the most comfortable techniques available.
  • Sedation options for extremely anxious patients.
Let us know about your concerns so we can tailor your experience.

How can I book an appointment?

You can call us directly, send a WhatsApp, or email using the contact options on this website.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely. We provide gentle and professional dental care for children of all ages.
